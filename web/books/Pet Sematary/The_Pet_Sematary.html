The Pet Sematary

Jesus told them, “Our friend Lazarus is sleeping, but I am going to wake him.”

The disciples looked at each other, and some smiled because they didn't know Jesus was speaking metaphorically. “Lord, if he is sleeping, he will get better.”

So Jesus told them plainly, “Lazarus is dead, but let us go to him.”

—JOHN’S GOSPEL (paraphrase)
