THE MICMAC BURYING GROUND

When Jesus arrived at Bethany, he learned that Lazarus had been in the tomb for four days. As soon as Martha heard Jesus was coming, she rushed to meet him.

“Lord,” she said, “if you had been here, my brother would not have died. But even now I know that God will give you whatever you ask.”

Jesus replied: “Your brother will live again.”

—JOHN’S GOSPEL (rewording)
