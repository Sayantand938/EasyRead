Dark Matter

Sinopsis

“Are you happy with your life?” These are the last words Jason Dessen hears before the masked person knocks him out. He wakes up strapped to a bed, surrounded by strangers in protective suits. A man Jason has never met smiles at him and says, “Welcome back, my friend.”

In this world he's woken up to, Jason’s life is not the one he knows. His wife is not his wife. His son was never born. And Jason is not just a college physics professor but a famous genius who has done something amazing--something impossible.

Is this world or the other one the dream? And even if the home he remembers is real, how can Jason ever get back to the family he loves? The answers are in a journey more amazing and scary than anything he could've imagined—one that will make him face the worst parts of himself as he fights a frightening enemy that seems impossible to beat.
