FACT:

The Priory of Sion—a European secret society started in 1099—is a real group. In 1975, the National Library of Paris found documents called Les Dossiers Secrets, listing many members of the Priory of Sion, like Sir Isaac Newton, Botticelli, Victor Hugo, and Leonardo da Vinci.

The Vatican group called Opus Dei is a very religious Catholic group that has been in the news lately because of stories about mind control, pressure, and a harmful practice called "corporal mortification." Opus Dei just finished building a $47 million world headquarters at 243 Lexington Avenue in New York City.

All descriptions of artwork, buildings, documents, and secret customs in this book are correct.
