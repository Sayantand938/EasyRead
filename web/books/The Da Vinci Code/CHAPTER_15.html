The Da Vinci Code

CHAPTER 15

It was time.

Silas felt strong as he stepped from the black Audi, the nighttime breeze rustling his loose robe. The winds of change are in the air. He knew the task before him would need care, not force, and he left his handgun in the car. The thirteen-round Heckler Koch USP 40 had been given to him by the Teacher.

A weapon of death has no place in a house of God.

The plaza before the great church was empty at this hour. The only people visible on the far side of Place Saint-Sulpice were a couple of teenage hookers showing what they had to offer to the late night tourists. Their young bodies stirred a familiar longing in Silas. His thigh flexed without him thinking, causing the barbed cilice belt to cut painfully into his flesh.

The lust disappeared instantly. For ten years now, Silas had faithfully denied himself all sexual pleasure, even masturbation. It was The Way. He knew he had given up much to follow Opus Dei, but he had gotten much more in return. A vow of celibacy and giving up all personal wealth hardly seemed like a sacrifice. Considering the poverty he had come from and the sexual horrors he had suffered in prison, celibacy was a welcome change. Now, having returned to France for the first time since being arrested and sent to prison in Andorra, Silas could feel his homeland testing him, dragging violent memories from his saved soul. You have been reborn, he reminded himself. His service to God today had required the sin of murder, and it was a sacrifice Silas knew he would have to hold silently in his heart forever. The measure of your faith is the measure of the pain you can endure, the Teacher had told him. Silas knew pain well and felt eager to prove himself to the Teacher, the one who had assured him his actions were ordered by a higher power.

"Hago la obra de Dios," Silas whispered, moving now toward the church entrance. Pausing in the shadow of the massive doorway, he took a deep breath. It was not until this moment that he truly realized what he was about to do, and what awaited him inside. The keystone. It will lead us to our final goal.

He raised his ghost-white fist and banged three times on the door.

Moments later, the bolts of the enormous wooden door began to move.
