Angels and Demons

Chapter 132

The camerlegno lay curled up on the dirt floor in front of St. Peter’s tomb. The Necropolis was cold, but it helped stop the blood flowing from the wounds he had made on himself. His Holiness would not find him here. Nobody would find him here…

“It is complicated,” the Pope’s voice echoed in his mind. “I will need time to help you understand…”

But the camerlegno knew no amount of time could make him understand.

Liar! I believed in you! GOD believed in you!

With a single sentence, the Pope had destroyed the camerlegno’s world. Everything the camerlegno had ever believed about his mentor was broken before his eyes. The truth stabbed into the camerlegno’s heart so hard that he stumbled backward out of the Pope’s office and vomited in the hallway.

“Wait!” the Pope had cried, chasing after him. “Please let me explain!”

But the camerlegno ran off. How could His Holiness expect him to take any more? Oh, the horrible wickedness of it! What if someone else found out? Imagine the dishonor to the church! Did the Pope’s holy promises mean nothing?

The madness came quickly, screaming in his ears, until he awoke before St. Peter’s tomb. It was then that God came to him with great power.

YOURS IS A VENGEFUL GOD!

Together, they made their plans. Together they would protect the church. Together they would bring faith back to this faithless world. Evil was everywhere. And yet the world had become used to it! Together they would reveal the darkness for the world to see… and God would win! Horror and Hope. Then the world would believe!

God’s first test had been easier than the camerlegno thought. Sneaking into the Pope’s bedroom… filling his syringe… covering the liar’s mouth as his body shook violently and died. In the moonlight, the camerlegno could see that the Pope’s wild eyes held something he wanted to say.

But it was too late.

The Pope had said enough.
