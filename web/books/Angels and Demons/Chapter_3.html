Angels and Demons

Chapter 3

Thousands of miles away, two men were meeting. The room was dark. Like something from the Middle Ages. Made of stone.

“Benvenuto,” the man in charge said. He was sitting in the shadows, hidden from view. “Were you successful?”

“Si,” the dark figure replied. “Perfectamente.” His words were as hard as the stone walls.

“And there will be no question about who is responsible?”

“None.”

“Excellent. Do you have what I asked for?”

The killer’s eyes gleamed, black like oil. He took out a heavy electronic device and put it on the table.

The man in the shadows seemed happy. “You have done well.”

“Serving the brotherhood is an honor,” the killer said.

Phase two starts soon. Get some rest. Tonight we change the world.
