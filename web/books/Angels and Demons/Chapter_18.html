Angels and Demons

Chapter 18

The woman’s hands were tied, her wrists now purple and swollen from rubbing. The dark-skinned Hassassin lay beside her, finished, admiring his naked prize. He wondered if she was really asleep, or just faking it to avoid serving him again.

He didn't care. He had gotten enough reward. Satisfied, he sat up in bed.

In his country, women were property. Weak. Pleasure tools. Goods to be traded like animals. And they knew their place. But here, in Europe, women pretended to be strong and independent, which both amused and excited him. Forcing them to submit physically was a pleasure he always enjoyed.

Now, despite feeling satisfied, the Hassassin felt another hunger growing inside him. He had killed last night, killed and cut up a body, and for him, killing was like heroin… each time only satisfying him for a while before making him want more. The excitement had faded. The craving had returned.

He looked at the sleeping woman beside him. Running his hand across her neck, he felt excited knowing he could end her life in a second. What would it matter? She was less than human, just a thing for pleasure and service. His strong fingers closed around her throat, enjoying her delicate pulse. Then, fighting the urge, he took his hand away. There was work to do. Service to something more important than his own desire.

As he got out of bed, he gloried in the honor of the job ahead. He still couldn't understand the power of this man named Janus and the ancient group he led. Amazingly, the group had chosen him. Somehow they had found out about his hatred… and his skills. He would never know how. Their roots reach wide.

Now they had given him the greatest honor. He would be their hands and their voice. Their killer and their messenger. The one his people knew as Malak al-haq - the Angel of Truth.
