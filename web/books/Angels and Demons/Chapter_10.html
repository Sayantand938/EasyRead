Angels and Demons

Chapter 10

The alley was narrow and deserted. The Hassassin walked faster, his black eyes eager. As he neared his goal, Janus’s last words came back to him. Phase two begins soon. Get some rest.

The Hassassin smirked. He had been awake all night, but sleep was the last thing he wanted. Sleep was for the weak. He was a warrior like those before him, and his people never slept once a battle started. This battle had definitely begun, and he had the honor of drawing first blood. Now he had two hours to enjoy his victory before working again.

Sleep? There are better ways to relax…

A taste for pleasure was something his ancestors passed down. They had used hashish, but he liked a different kind of satisfaction. He took pride in his body - a finely tuned, deadly machine that he refused to pollute with drugs, despite his background. He had found a better addiction than drugs… a healthier and more rewarding one.

Feeling that familiar excitement growing, the Hassassin hurried down the alley. He reached the plain door and rang the bell. A small opening in the door appeared, and two soft brown eyes looked him up and down. Then the door opened.

“Welcome,” the well-dressed woman said. She led him into a perfectly furnished sitting room with dim lights. The air smelled of expensive perfume and musk. “Whenever you’re ready.” She gave him a book of photos. “Ring me when you’ve made your choice.” Then she left.

The Hassassin smiled.

Sitting on the soft couch with the photo album on his lap, he felt a deep hunger grow. Though his people did not celebrate Christmas, he thought this must be how a Christian child felt, sitting before a pile of Christmas gifts, about to see the wonders inside. He opened the album and looked at the photos. A lifetime of sexual dreams looked back at him.

Marisa. An Italian beauty. Passionate. A young Sophia Loren.

Sachiko. A Japanese geisha. Slim. Surely talented.

Kanara. A striking black beauty. Strong. Exotic.

He looked through the entire album twice and made his choice. He pressed a button on the table next to him. A minute later, the woman who had welcomed him came back. He pointed to his choice. She smiled. “Follow me.”

After settling the payment, the woman made a quiet phone call. She waited a few minutes and then led him up a winding marble staircase to a fancy hallway. “It’s the gold door at the end,” she said. “You have expensive taste.”

I should, he thought. I know quality.

The Hassassin walked quietly down the hallway like a panther waiting for a meal. When he reached the door, he smiled. It was already partly open… inviting him in. He pushed, and the door opened silently.

When he saw his choice, he knew he had chosen well. She was exactly as he had asked… naked, lying on her back, her arms tied to the bedposts with thick velvet cords.

He crossed the room and ran a dark finger across her pale stomach. I killed last night, he thought. You are my reward.
