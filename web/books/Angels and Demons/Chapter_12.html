Angels and Demons

Chapter 12

In another country, a young guard sat patiently in front of a large bank of video monitors. He watched as images flashed by—live feeds from hundreds of wireless video cameras that watched over the large complex. The images passed in an endless stream.

An ornate hallway.

A private office.

An industrial-size kitchen.

As the pictures went by, the guard tried not to daydream. He was nearing the end of his shift, but he remained watchful. Service was an honor. Someday he would receive his ultimate reward.

As his thoughts wandered, an image caught his attention. Suddenly, with a quick jerk that surprised even himself, he reached out and pressed a button on the control panel. The picture froze.

His nerves on edge, he leaned closer to the screen to get a better look. The monitor showed that the image was from camera #86—a camera that was supposed to be watching a hallway.

But the image was definitely not a hallway.
