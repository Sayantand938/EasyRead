Angels and Demons

Chapter 57

The director of CERN, Maximilian Kohler, opened his eyes, feeling the cool medicine flow through him, opening his airways. He was breathing normally again. He was in a private room in the CERN infirmary, his wheelchair next to the bed.

He looked around, noticing the paper robe they had put on him. His clothes were folded on the chair. He heard a nurse outside. He stayed there, listening. Then, quietly, he reached for his clothes. Struggling, he dressed. Then he pulled himself into his wheelchair.

Quietly coughing, he went to the door. He moved the wheels himself, not using the motor. He looked out. The hall was empty.

Silently, Maximilian Kohler left the infirmary.
