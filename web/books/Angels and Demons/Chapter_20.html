Chapter 20

P hase two, the Hassassin thought, walking into the dark tunnel.

The torch in his hand was too much. He knew that. But it was for show. Show was everything. Fear, he had learned, was his friend. Fear stops faster than any weapon.

There was no mirror in the passage to admire his disguise, but he could tell from the shadow of his robe that he looked perfect. Fitting in was part of the plan… part of the evil plot. He never thought he would play this part.

Two weeks ago, he would have thought the task ahead of him impossible. A suicide mission. Walking naked into a lion’s den. But Janus had changed what impossible meant.

Janus had shared many secrets with the Hassassin in the last two weeks… including this tunnel. Old, but still easy to travel through.

As he got closer to his enemy, the Hassassin wondered if what waited for him inside would be as easy as Janus had said. Janus had told him someone on the inside would make the needed plans. Someone on the inside. Amazing. The more he thought about it, the more he knew it was easy.
